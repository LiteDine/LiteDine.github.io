What does LiteDine do?

Our app provides various details from restaurants when you search such as good type, rating, and even the restaurant's menu. You can even see whether the chosen restaurant supports your current diet (e.g. vegan, gluten-free, etc.) These are only a few among many other search details LiteDine provides so you know what you're ordering.

What does it look like?

When you open the app, you will be greeted by a simple and user-friendly interface to navigate through our app. It's simple! There's a big search bar right in the middle, so you can pick and choose through what you feel like searching for, and then you press search as our algorithm does the magic!

You get boxes that pop up on your screen that show details of each restaurant, and you can hide them from the screen to show a map behind them! The map gives you directions to each restaurant so you won't get lost on your way there. LiteDine is committed to giving users the best possible experience using our app.
