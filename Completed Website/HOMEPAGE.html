What is LiteDine?

LiteDine is the all in one on-the-go dining experience! Our app allows you to filter through restaurants and their menus based on your diet, or food preferences. This app is a lifesaver for tourists and any person on a diet.

Who are we?

Here at Team LiteDine, we all share a common goal. We want to remove the hassle from eating at a restaurant while on a diet, and make it easier to find the right restaurant while traveling.
